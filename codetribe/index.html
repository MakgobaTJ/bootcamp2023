CR Ramaphosa

Matamela Cyril Ramaphosa (born 17 November 1952) is a South African businessman and politician who is currently serving as
the fifth democratically elected president of South Africa. Formerly an anti-apartheid activist, trade union leader, and businessman, Ramaphosa is also the president of the African National Congress (ANC).
